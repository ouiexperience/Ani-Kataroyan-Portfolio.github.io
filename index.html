About Me.

I’m Ani Kataroyan — a Learning Experience Designer with a background in instructional design, e-learning development, and educational storytelling. I specialize in crafting learning programs that drive measurable results using tools like Articulate Storyline, Rise 360, Canva, and LMS platforms. With over 8 years of experience designing engaging curriculum for diverse learners, I’ve led end-to-end course creation — from needs analysis and SME collaboration to content design, implementation, and evaluation. My projects have spanned onboarding, product knowledge, behavioral skills, and customer-facing service training. I bring a builder’s mindset to every project: breaking down complexity, focusing on learner behavior, and balancing speed with scalability. I’m passionate about using data, feedback, and design thinking to build high-impact learning that empowers teams and supports business goals. I’m excited to bring this agile, learner-centered approach to Amazon’s Ring Global Learning and Quality team to help create world-class learning experiences for associates worldwide.
